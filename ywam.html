Vidalife Cancun is a long time friend of YWAM Cancun. If you're interested in missionary training and would love to live in Cancun, YWAM is a great opportunity.

Youth With A Mission (YWAM) is an interdenominational international missionary organization. In the past 50 years YWAM has mobilized four million short, medium and long term workers to missionary service, carrying out evangelism, missionary training and humanitarian works on five continents.

YWAM has 1,200+ bases of operations in 173 countries. In YWAM Cancun there are people from different nations working hand in hand. In everything YWAM missionaries do, the passion is to know God and to make Him known.

If you're passionate about serving, YWAM offers a five-month course in Missionary Discipleship Training. For more information, contact us or visit their website: ywamcancun.org
