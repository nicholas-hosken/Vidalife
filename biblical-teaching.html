BIBLICAL TEACHING

We believe that the Bible is God's Word to all men. It is the source of all truth and wisdom written under the supernatural guidance of the Holy Spirit. It is the ultimate resource in life, and we teach our congregations to seek God through His word and the example of Jesus' teaching.

Our sermons are usually preached in English and Spanish simultaneously. We enjoy this integration of cultures into one Sunday service as a glimpse of Heaven.
