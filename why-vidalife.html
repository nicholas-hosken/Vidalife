WHY VIDALIFE?


As the people of God, we are committed to being a community for anyone searching for truth. We believe in serving people inside and outside of the church. We believe that the Bible is the inspired perfect Word of God, as it contains the only truth and supreme authority in matters of faith and living.

We believe that church is a movement, not an establishment. People are brought into the church through the work of God and, therefore, are there to serve a purpose.

We believe that salvation is not about standing aside from the world, but rather standing apart in the world. And, this too, is the work of the Holy Spirit of God in the believer.
