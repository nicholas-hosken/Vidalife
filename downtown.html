DOWNTOWN CANCUN


Centrally located in Market 28. Family-oriented church. On Sundays, Vidalife Downtown is a place where people from around the world gather to worship God and enjoy time together. Being a part of this community impacts lives and reminds us that we can't do life alone.

If you are in town for a short trip, we invite you to visit us on Sunday to worship together.

The downtown congregation also offers missionary internship opportunities for individuals or families. We would love to share with you the internship plan when you come to visit.
