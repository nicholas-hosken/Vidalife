WELCOME TO VIDALIFE CANCUN

Vidalife Church is a dynamic, vibrant and growing community of believers with 4 mission sites around Cancun, Mexico. Our downtown location is easy to get to from anywhere in Cancun, so we will be happy for your visit. Come worship with us on Sunday mornings at 11:00. Visitors and newcomers are always welcome at Vidalife Cancun. Whether you're on vacation, new to the area, or have lived here for many years, we'd love to meet you!

Come join us as we communicate the gospel, cultivate disciples, create unity, and celebrate salvation.
