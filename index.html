WELCOME TO VIDALIFE CANCUN
 "UNTIL WE ALL COME INTO THE UNITY OF THE FAITH"
Vidalife Church is a dynamic, vibrant and growing community of believers with 4 mission sites around Cancun, Mexico. Our downtown location is easy to get to from anywhere in Cancun, so we’ll be happy for your visit. Come worship with us on Sunday mornings at 11:00. Visitors and newcomers are always welcome at Vidalife Cancun. Whether you're on vacation, new to the area, or have lived here for many years, we'd love to meet you!

Come join us as we communicate the gospel, cultivate disciples, create unity, and celebrate salvation.


VIDALIFE MISSIONS



VIDALIFE DOWNTOWN
Pastor Noé Camacho

Centrally located in Market 28. Family-oriented church. On Sundays, Vidalife Downtown is a place where people from around the world gather to worship God and enjoy time together. Being in community impacts lives and reminds us that we can't do life alone.
MILAGRO MISSION
Pastor Benny Lopez

Located on the outskirts of Cancun, this community is not a sunny paradise by any means. Here we serve families with scarce living conditions.
MILAGRO MISSION 2
Pastor Victor Mendez

An even poorer part of the Milagro community, where there is a great need to give children hope and a sense of belonging.
ESFUERZO MISSION SITE
Pastor Alex Bernal

An inner-city community in Cancun with 3rd-world problems, but a great hope in a Great God.
