WELCOME TO VIDALIFE CANCUN
 "Church motto goes here."
Vidalife, Is A Dynamic, Vibrant And Growing Church Based On Outskirts Of Cancun's Famous Market 28. Our Mandate Is To Win The Lost At Any Cost And A Mission Of Mending The Nets Throughout The Yucatan By Loving God, Loving People And Building The Local Church.

Visitors And Newcomers Are Always Welcome At Iglesia Vidalife Cancun. Whether You're Just Passing Through, New To The Area Or Have Lived Here For Many Years, We'd Love To Meet You!

We Meet At 11:00am On A Sunday For Our Family Service. You Are Always More Than Welcome To Join Us.
